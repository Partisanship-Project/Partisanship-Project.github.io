  • How does it work?

    We have already done the calculations for the partisanship being expressed by the candidates in House and Senate races this year. All you need to do is enter in the name of the candidate you want to find in the box above and click "Search." We'll show you how the candidate's average partisanship has evolved this year and the partisan words they've used most this year.

  • How do you do it?

    We use machine learning algorithms to identify the words and phrases Democrats and Republicans use on Twitter and measure how partisan those words are. Words that are used more frequently by Republicans are systematically different than words used more frequently by Democrats. This allows us to observe the intensity of a candidate's expressed partisanship based on the frequency of their use of partisan words.
